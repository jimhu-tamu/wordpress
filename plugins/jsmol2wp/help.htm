About

This Wordpress plugin is based on the JSmol molecular graphics viewer from Bob Hanson, Zhou Renjian, and Takanori Nakane. Adaptation of JSmol to a Wordpress plugin was started by Jim Hu in June 2014.

JSmol2wp is still in early development!!! Use at your own risk. For now, bug reports should be sent to jim.hu.biobio@gamail or to the JMol mailing list. Or report them to @jimhu via twitter.
